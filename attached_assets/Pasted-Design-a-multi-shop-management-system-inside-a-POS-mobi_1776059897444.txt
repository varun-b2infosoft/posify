Design a multi-shop management system inside a POS mobile app that allows:

Managing multiple shops
Assigning staff (salesman, manager, admin)
Setting role-based permissions
Transferring stock between shops
Tracking stock transfer status visually
Switching shop context globally (dashboard filter)

The UI should feel structured, powerful, but not overwhelming.

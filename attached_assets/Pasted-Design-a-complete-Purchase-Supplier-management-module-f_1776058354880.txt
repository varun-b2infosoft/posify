Design a complete Purchase & Supplier management module for a POS mobile app.

This module should allow users to:

Manage suppliers (add, edit, delete, select)
Create purchase orders
Refill stock for existing products
Track purchase history

The experience should feel fast, structured, and business-focused, not form-heavy.

               Last Purchase Date

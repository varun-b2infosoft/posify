Design a high-performance, touch-optimized POS (Sell Items) screen for a mobile application. This screen must prioritize speed, clarity, and minimal taps—users should be able to complete a sale in seconds.

🧱 Layout Structure
Split layout:
Top: Search + Quick Actions
Middle: Product Grid
Bottom: Cart Summary + Checkout
Sticky bottom checkout bar (always visible)
🔍 Section 1: Search & Quick Access
Large search bar:
Placeholder: “Search product / Scan barcode”
Barcode scan icon inside search
Filter / category button next to search

💡 Design:

Rounded input
Slight shadow
Instant feedback typing
🗂️ Section 2: Category Tabs
Horizontal scroll tabs:
All, Beverages, Snacks, Dairy, etc.
Active tab highlighted

💡 UX:

Quick switching without reload lag
Sticky below search
🛍️ Section 3: Product Grid
Grid layout (2 columns mobile)
Each product card includes:
Product image
Name (1 line max)
Price (₹)
Stock indicator (small text or dot)

💡 Interaction:

Tap = add to cart instantly
Long press = open product detail/edit quantity

💡 Design:

Clean cards
Slight elevation
Fast tap feedback
🛒 Section 4: Cart Panel (Bottom Section)

Sticky, always visible.

Shows:
Total items count
Total price (bold, large)
Expandable cart preview

When expanded:

List of added items:
Name
Quantity controls (+ / -)
Price per item
Remove (trash icon)

💡 UX:

Quantity change should be instant (no lag)
Swipe to delete (optional but slick)
💳 Section 5: Checkout Actions

Inside bottom bar:

Primary Button: “Checkout”
Secondary quick buttons:
Hold Order
Clear Cart
Apply Discount

💡 Design:

Checkout button = dominant (full width or large)
Use strong color (green or primary brand)
💰 Section 6: Payment Modal (After Checkout Tap)

Slide-up modal with:

Total Amount (big, bold)
Payment methods:
Cash
UPI
Card
Input field (for cash received)
Auto calculate change

💡 UX:

One-tap payment selection
Show success state immediately
⚡ Speed Features (Critical)
Add product in 1 tap
Checkout in 2–3 taps max
No unnecessary confirmations
Smooth animations (<200ms)
⚠️ Edge UX Cases
Out of stock → disable or show warning
Low stock → small alert badge
Duplicate add → increase quantity instead of new row
🎨 Design Style
Clean, minimal, zero clutter
Colors:
Background: light neutral
Primary: deep blue/purple
CTA: green (checkout)
Typography:
Big numbers for price
Simple readable font
🧠 Advanced UX (Optional but Powerful)
Show “Recent Products” at top
Add “Favorites” category
Haptic feedback on add-to-cart
Offline mode indicator
🔥 Core Philosophy

This screen is not for beauty—it’s for speed and survival.
Every extra tap is lost money. Every delay is frustration.
